## Which file matters most

`content-pillars.md` is the most important file in this folder. The content research and content ideas skills score every trend and every draft against your three pillars and the Uncopyable Core checklist inside it. If that file is blank, the scoring has nothing to measure against and the whole system returns noise. Fill it in first.

## How they work together

The content system reads `content-pillars.md` plus `positioning.md` and `my-voice-dna.md` together before writing or scoring anything. `business-profile.md` is the baseline identity every output lives inside. `proof-and-results.md` supplies real evidence when content needs it. `patterns.md` and `current-priorities.md` calibrate how the system helps you and what it pushes toward.

Update these files in place as your business changes. They are living documents, not one-time setup.
