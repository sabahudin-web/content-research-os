## What belongs here

- Content angles that performed above average, and why
- Post type patterns (story vs. carousel vs. contrarian, which converts for which audience)
- Topics that generated qualified leads vs. just engagement
- Newsletter patterns (which editions drove signups vs. replies)
- Video lessons (what framing held attention or drove traffic)
